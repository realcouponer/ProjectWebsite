The Superalgos Trading League is split in two: Minor League and Major League. Each league is subdivided in divisions.

Each division runs longer Competitions with increasing capital requirements and larger awards in prize money. who compete well in minor divisions may chose to discretionarily move to upper divisions.

Core Features

  • Each runs its own league.

  • Competition events across all participating Exchanges run exactly at the same datetime. This helps competing Algo-Makers to plan ahead and participate in multiple Competitions across different Exchanges.

  • It also allows the audience to enjoy Competitions and keep track of them across different Exchanges.

  • New Community members have free access to a wealth of knowledge and trading technology.

  • Adding value by improving the ecosystem's is consequently incentivized. Competition winners get to multiply their capital by a factor of e.g. 10X in the 9th Division, 5X in the 8th Division, 3X in the 7th, 3X in the 6th and 2X in Major Leagues.

  • Prize money for the winner of each division added to the original capital requirement is enough to meet the capital requirement of the immediate higher division, promoting mobility across the league.

  • At the end of every Competition, the winning Algorithms are open-sourced within the in order to enable the upgrade of the ecosystem's intelligence. This allows to use the state-of-the-art as a starting point for building the next generation of Algorithms for the next Competitions to come.

  • The ecosystem is based on a comprehensive evolutionary model involving a form of artificial life by which Algorithms become autonomous living organisms.
