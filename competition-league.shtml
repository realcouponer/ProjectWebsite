The following description corresponds to an envisioned league structure that is not yet implemented. Current Alpha-stage Competitions are structured similar to the 10th Division in the below table.

The Superalgos Trading League is split in two: Minor League and Major League. Each league is subdivided in divisions.

Each division runs longer Competitions with increasing capital requirements and larger awards in prize money. who compete well in minor divisions may chose to discretionarily move to upper divisions.

Core Features

  • Each runs its own league.

  • Competition events across all participating Exchanges run exactly at the same datetime. This helps competing to plan ahead and participate in multiple Competitions across different Exchanges.

  • It also allows the audience to enjoy Competitions and keep track of them across different Exchanges.

  • Community members gain free access to a wealth of knowledge and trading technology, including state-of-the-art and .

  • Adding value by improving the ecosystem's Algorithms is decisively incentivized. Competition winners may multiply their capital several times depending on the division they chose to compete in.

  • Prize money for the winner of each division added to the original capital requirement is enough to meet the capital requirement of the immediate higher division, promoting mobility across the league.

  • At the end of every Competition, the winning Algorithms are open-sourced within the Community in order to enable the upgrade of the ecosystem's intelligence. This allows Algo-Makers to use the state-of-the-art as a starting point for building the next generation of Algorithms for the next Competitions to come.
