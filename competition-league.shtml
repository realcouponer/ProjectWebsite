Trading League Structure

The following description corresponds to an envisioned league structure that is not yet implemented. Alpha-stage Competitions are structured similar to the 10th Division in the below table.

are structured in a multi-layer league system designed to drive evolution towards trading increasingly important value in assets and/or capital. Beyond performance and reliability, it is the capacity to trade long-term and with large capitals that make marketable.

The Superalgos Trading League is split in two: Minor Leagues and Major Leagues. Each league is subdivided into divisions.

Subsequent divisions run longer Competitions with increasing capital requirements and larger awards in prize money. Increasing capital requirements and longer Competitions is the funnel that drives evolution towards the desired outcome of marketable Strategies, crucial for Stage 3.

may move discretionarily throughout available divisions. The assortment of different types of Competitions provides choices, making the activity attractive to both professionals and amateurs alike.

Each runs its league. Competition events across all participating Exchanges run exactly at the same DateTime. This helps competing Algo-Makers to plan and participate in multiple Competitions across different Exchanges. It also allows the audience to enjoy Competitions and keep track of them across different Exchanges.

Exchanges compete for traders in the open market, therefore, they strive to offer competitive cash prizes for Competition winners to attract more participants.

Core Features

  • Community members gain free access to a wealth of knowledge and trading technology, including state-of-the-art Strategies and .

  • Adding value by improving the ecosystem's Algorithms is decisively incentivized. Competition winners may multiply their capital several times depending on the division they chose to compete in.

  • Prize money for the winner of each division added to the original capital requirement is enough to meet the capital requirement of the immediate higher division, promoting mobility across the league.

  • At the end of every Competition, the winning Strategies are open-sourced among competitors to enable the upgrade of the ecosystem's intelligence. This allows Algo-Makers to use the state-of-the-art as a starting point for building the next generation of Strategies for the next Competitions to come.
