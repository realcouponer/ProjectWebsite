  • The Solution, Part 2: EVOLUTION

    The Role of the Community

    • Train: teams of developers, traders and analysts build and train trading bots.
    • Compete: they put bots to compete for cash prizes, fun, honor and glory.
    • Fork: once each competition ends, code is opened and teams fork the winners.
    • Repeat: winners are improved and the new generation is put to compete again.

  • The Solution, Part 3: FINANCIAL LIFE

    The Role of Machines

    • Financial beings: once released, bots become living stakeholders in the ecosystem.
    • Autonomy: financial beings are autonomous agents, independent of their creators.
    • Value-oriented: bots strive to produce value to attract subscribers for their services.
    • Survival of the fittest: top performers thrive; inefficient bots run out of ALGO and die.

  • The Solution, Part 4: ALGO Token

    The Role of the Environment

    • Payments: bots pay / earn micro fees for the services they consume / offer.
    • Energy: a form of energy turning bots into beings with a financial life of their own.
    • Scarcity: regulates the numbers of financial beings in the environment.
    • Free market: floating prices enable market forces to affect viability of bots.
