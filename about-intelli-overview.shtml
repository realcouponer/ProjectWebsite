The Gradual Emergence of Specific Use Cases
STAGE 1
Automate Trading

In the future, users of Superalgos may choose to rent computational resources to other users.

Suppliers of such services will be users with a technical background that may guarantee a reliable service.

Consumers of such services will be users who would rather not handle their own data-mining operation and execution environment to run trading sessions, but would rather prefer to hire a hosted service instead.

The rental of computational resources at a shall be paid with the native token.

Also in the future, users of Superalgos running data mines may choose to expose to the Superalgos Network, a peer-to-peer network connecting instances of the Superalgos software, yet to be deployed. Superalgos users who choose to connect to the network may consume these data services.

Data services provided through the network shall be paid with the native token.

STAGE 2
Evolve Trading Intelligence

In the future, the Superalgos Project will promote an evolutionary model by which the open-sourcing of intelligence will be incentivized.

Users who choose to participate will bring part of their intelligence forward to compete in trading competitions hosted at exchanges. They will do so incentivized by hefty cash prices. Once competitions finish, all participating intelligence will be open-sourced.

The community will then access the newly open-sourced intelligence and will work to improve it so as to bring it back to compete again in the next round of competitions.

The above is part of the evolutionary model that will be implemented in the future, and that will generate a body of ever-evolving open-source intelligence that anyone may access.

To participate in , users will pay entry , which shall be payable in the native token.

Official competition will be payable in the native token too.

STAGE 3
Make Intelligence Accessible

In the future, the Superalgos Network to be launched in Stage 1 will evolve to host a marketplace of different kinds of trading intelligence. Users of Superalgos will be able to expose their own intelligence in the form of data services, trading services, and asset management services.

Different types of users will specialize in different activities, and will consume each other's products and services to complement and augment their intelligence.

Under such future scenario, even investors not participating in the creation of intelligence will use Superalgos to access the intelligence of others.

The will be a free and permissionless ecosystem existing within a peer-to-peer network of users using Superalgos.
