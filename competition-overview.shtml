Why We Compete

What kind of new disruptive system could produce zero cost, ever-evolving trading intelligence, that would sooner or later outperform the intelligence produced in silos by investment companies? A glimpse into the existing model and the Superalgos alternative...


The Status Quo

Investment companies keep trading technology in absolute secrecy. The assumption is made that this gives them an edge over other market participants. It is also assumed that lose performance when used too broadly. Even within a company, specialists work alone or in small groups. Evolution of trading intelligence is therefore capped by the intelligence of small groups.

Our Open Evolutionary Approach

A global collective creates Algorithms and puts them to compete for prize money, reputation and fun! After each ends, Algorithms are open-sourced. The collective improves the best ones and puts the new generation back to compete again, unleashing a relentless evolutionary process. Algorithms losing performance are quickly replaced by improved evolutions.

Superalgos Trading League

The mid-term goal of the project is producing the required shift in incentives that will drive the to openly collaborate and evolve trading intelligence. Instead of focusing only on profits obtained from trading, the Community participates in , attracted by several incentives...

Partnership with Exchanges

The Superalgos Organization partners with Exchanges in the following terms...

  • The enables Exchanges to run their own Trading Leagues.

  • Competitions are expected to generate substantial trading volume at Exchanges.

  • Exchanges promote Competitions among their clients and award to the winner as prize money to incentivize participation and thus grow trading volume.

  • As each Exchange is on-boarded and set up with its Competition, the Exchange lists the Intelli token so that clients can cash out awarded Intelli tokens if they wish so.

  • Once clients of each newly on-boarded Exchange become aware of the Superalgos Project, a small Exchange offering of Intelli tokens is launched at the Exchange.

Exchanges' Gains

  • Competitions drive trading volume on which Exchanges charge Transaction Fees.

  • Transaction Fees finance the prize money awarded by Exchanges to incentivize Competitions and also generate profits.

  • Exchanges have the opportunity to raise and increase awareness within the growing Superalgos Community, and compete for new customers.

  • Exchanges become invested stakeholders and get an —a device that issues Intelli tokens to the Stream beneficiary address at a certain rate—in compensation for the value added to the project.

  • The gamification (Competitions) and algorithmic-trading toolbox provided by Superalgos activate traders and drive an increase of trading volume within the existing Exchange clients.

Superalgos Project Gains

  • Exchanges contribute to bootstrapping the driving new members to the Superalgos Project and incentivizing the Community to compete by awarding significant prize money to competition winners.

  • Exchanges contribute to the funding and growth of the Superalgos Project by listing, marketing and conducting Exchange offerings of the Intelli token.
