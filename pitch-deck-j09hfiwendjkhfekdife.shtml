• Superalgos


    An open-source project with the vision of a global
    Collective Trading Intelligence at the service of all people.

  • The Status Quo

    No one shares their Algorithms
    or know-how.

    • Investment companies keep trading technology in absolute secrecy.
    • The assumption is made that this gives them an edge over other market participants.
    • It is also assumed that Algorithms lose performance when used too broadly.
    • Even within a company, specialists work alone or in small groups.
    • Evolution of trading intelligence is therefore capped by the intelligence of small groups.

  • Our Approach

    Challenge the silos
    with global collaboration.

    • A global collective creates trading Algorithms → uncapped group size!
    • Algorithms participate in Competitions for prize money and reputation → shift in incentives!
    • After each Competition, Algorithms are open-sourced → worldwide knowledge dissemination!
    • The collective improves the best Algorithms and puts them to compete again → rapid evolution!

  • The Expected Outcome

    A Collective Trading Intelligence alongside proprietary solutions,
    coexisting in a decentralized network hosting a Trading Intelligence Marketplace.

    • Our open-source trading intelligence will be distributed across a trustless peer-to-peer network.
    • The network will open to traditional investment companies and black-boxed intelligence too.
    • A global marketplace will provide various asset management services to investors of all calibers.
    • The system will be glued with sustainable monetary incentives to participate in the network.

  • Our Strategy

    We build towards our vision with a solid, down-to-earth,
    bottom-up approach, in 3 stages...

    • STAGE 1 is about attracting and empowering traders, and sowing the seed for cooperation.
    • STAGE 2 is about engaging the Community in an evolutionary model to increase intelligence.
    • STAGE 3 is about making trading intelligence accessible to as many as possible.

  • Stage 1: Automate Trading

    We have pre-released the Superalgos Desktop App,
    a free open-source tool that automates complete trading systems.

    • No coding required: visually analyze and optimize strategies, right over the charts.
    • Backtest, paper-trade and deploy strategies as fully automated Algorithms to trade live.
    • Share and engage in collaboration with friends, trading partners and the whole Community.
    • Easy installation in your machine: no need to trust third-parties with strategies, API keys or funds.

    Stage 1, ✔ checked!

  • Stage 2: Evolve Intelligence

    Next stage will bring the Superalgos Trading League,
    a Competition system designed to drive evolution and engage Exchanges.

    • Exchanges will host Superalgos Competitions to drive trading volume to their own businesses.
    • They will sponsor significant prize money to incentivize Algo-maker teams to compete.
    • Prize money will come at no cost to Exchanges as it will originate from standard volume discounts.
    • Exchanges will market Competitions to their clients, onboarding whole communities to Superalgos.

  • Stage 3: Marketplace

    As the network grows,
    intelligence will be put at the service of all people.

    • Investors of all calibers will rent asset management services offered throughout the network.
    • Asset managers will be professionals and automated solutions renting algos from Algo-makers.
    • Algo-makers counting traders, developers and data analysist will be paid for the use of their algos.
    • Constant evolution will guarantee diversity and prompt replacement of algos that lose their edge.

  • The Main Challenge

    Bootstrapping the project until it snowballs!

    • The project is wired around two parallel positive feedback loops that accelerate growth.
    • Both loops incentivize Algo-makers, the key to an ever-growing collective intelligence.

    Bootstrap Injection
    ↓
    More Prize Money More Trading Intelligence
    ↗ ↘                       ↗ ↘
    More Volume at Exchanges More Algo-Makers More Performance
    ↖ ↙                       ↖ ↙
    More Competitors More Demand for Rentals

  • Go to Market Strategy

    We leverage the partnership with Exchanges
    to onboard their communities, one by one as the project advances.

    1. Activate traders with the Desktop App, and help them trade more, creating volume.
    2. Onboard a minor Exchange to market Superalgos within their clients → deal signed!
    3. Host micro token offering at the Exchange and launch alpha-stage Competitions.
    4. Repeat with other Exchanges, targeting bigger ones as the Community matures.

  • Intellli Token (INTI)

    1.5 billion Intelli tokens may be issued during the first five years.
    For the sixth year and on, an issuing cap of 150 million Intelli per year is set.

    • A dynamic economy with a declining inflation rate, to support 3 main needs:
    1. Development: issuance is backed by value added to the project by people and companies.
    2. Liquidity: issuance through rolling IEOs is backed by user base growth.
    3. Intelligence: issuance to bootstrap initial prizes is backed by growth in intelligence.

    In all instances, issuance is driven by the growth of the ecosystem.

  • Token Utility

    The sole medium of payment throughout the network
    for the following use cases:

    1. Paying for Official Competition Tickets and Prizes.
    2. Paying for data, trading and asset management services at the Marketplace.
    3. Paying for computational resources (to run algos) rented at Nodes in the Network.
    4. Paying for bundled volume disccounts distributed back to Node Operators by Exchanges.
