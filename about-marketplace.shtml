Trading Intelligence Marketplace

The Marketplace runs as a distributed system on the , and represents the gateway for the to access the capital markets attracting traders, investors and asset managers.

In general terms, investors will subscribe the services of asset managers, who in turn will hire the trading intelligence available in the network.

Asset managers will be companies or individuals in the network and their services may vary in terms of sophistication according to the size of capitals they manage.

Asset management services may be as elaborate as available elsewhere in the industry or as simple as an application connecting the subscription directly to .

The network will offers both open source trading intelligence evolved within , but also close-source intelligence black-boxed within ' services, and kept private from the rest of the Community.



The flow of the Marketplace is the following...



1. The provides liquidity through Exchanges.

2. Investors (crypto-assets holders) buy Intelli at the Exchange.

3. Investors use Intelli to pay for Asset Managers' subscription services.

4. Asset Managers acquire trading intelligence from the network, subscribing to .

The Algorithm obtaining the subscription autonomously pays for all the services it consumes, such as:

  • Node Operators, who offer execution and data storage services to Algorithms;

  • Other agents providing data, signals or other forms of trading intelligence;

At the same time, Algorithms pay :

  • In the case of closed source, the revenue goes to the sole proprietor of the Algorithm.
