Further Opportunities for Developers

Are you a talented developer looking for a smart crypto project to contribute to at an early stage?

Superalgos is collective project bringing people together to breed trading algorithms.

There is both empirical and scientific evidence that show how groups can be empowered to unprecedented levels when provided with the precise set of information technology tools and organized in specific ways.

The Superalgos project is about enabling, incentivizing and building such a community of people while designing people's and machines' interactions in ways that are proven to increase the collective intelligence of the group in order to maximize the odds of success.

It's about creating and nurturing an open supermind that can eventually outperform the corporate entities currently dominating the markets.

The technology required to support these dynamics is currently under development by multiple teams, in a collaborative setting. If you are a talented developer willing to participate in an open source crypto-project, we are happy to invite you to join us.
