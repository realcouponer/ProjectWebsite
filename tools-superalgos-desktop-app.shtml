Design Principles

Built to fit your trading style and needs
Flexibility

  • It's a rules-based system that will do whatever you tell it to do.

  • You may freely design both highly complex and simple strategies alike.

  • You decide how to use the app, be it to test your strategies, to get signals while trading manually, or to fully automate your trading.

  • The app is cross platform thus works on Windows, Mac OS and Linux systems.

Accessibility

  • No coding is required as you set rules using simple math and logical statements.

  • It's a visual app displaying strategies in action directly over the charts.

  • You may use shared strategies to get up and running in no time.

  • A logical framework guides you in designing your strategies.

  • Installation is easy, with quality documentation and educational material.

Extendibility

  • Strategies are portable and conform to a trading protocol.

  • You may seamlessly collaborate with colleagues and share your work.

  • If you can code you'll dive deeper and come up with your own hacks!

  • Developers may add functionality and build complementary apps.

  • An ecosystem of complementary apps will eventually grow around the protocol.

Execution Modes

Top Testing and Live Trading Capabilitites


Advanced Backtests

  • Set up unlimited backtesting sessions, each with their own parameters including time range, fees and slippage assumptions, time period, and more.

  • Visualize and analyze individual trades right on the charts.

  • Test multiple rule variations all at once and compare results visually over the charts.

  • Avoid overfitting exercising full control over how you use your data sets.



Paper Trade & Forward-Tests

  • Run strategies forwards without placing orders at the exchange to verify efficacy.

  • Test strategies forward placing orders at the exchange for a configurable fraction of your balance to validate slippage assumptions and overall execution.

  • Enjoy the same visual aides as with backtesting to analyze trades right over the charts.



Live Trading

  • Live-trade with multiple strategies from within the same instance of the app and the same machine.

  • Use multiple accounts or sub-accounts at the exchange, each with their own API keys.

Did we mention you can run all of these testing and live trading modes simultaneously?
