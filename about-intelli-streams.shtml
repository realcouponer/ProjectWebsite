Bitcoin borrowed the mining analogy from the mining industry as a way to convey the notion that securing the network requires significant investments of various resources, starting with the initial cost of the mining rig and infrastructure along with ongoing operational costs.

Entities willing to make such investments and provide such valuable service to the network would in turn be compensated with newly minted bitcoin.

It is in that same spirit that we invent our own concept of earning and keeping an Intelli Stream. The analogy describes how any person or company may help secure the success of the Superalgos Project, and obtain Intelli Tokens in reward, on a continuous basis.

In nature the water springs up in places that connect to the abundant water source, and forms streams that flow and promote life along their path.

We imagine the Intelli source as the source of energy in the , connecting to an entity that proves its willingness to reach out by contributing value. In return, the ecosystem offers a spring from which a stream of Intelli will flow.

The Intelli Stream is potentially endless and will last for as long as the contributor's pledges are honoured.

We wish to form lasting and mutually rewarding connections.

Like with bitcoin miners, earning an Intelli Stream requires an upfront investment, in our case, mostly of time and effort, along with an ongoing commitment of similar resources in the long run.

Intelli Streams

An Intelli Stream is a soon to be deployed Ethereum smart contract, and the instrument to be used to distribute Intelli among all the entities that add value to the Superalgos Project.


Intelli Allocation


The incentives scheme is designed to satisfy a fundamental requirement: keep key stakeholders incentivized throughout the life of the project, meaning both in the short term and in the long run.

Intelli Streams have two main features:

  • allocate a perpetual stream of tokens to a beneficiary Intelli address;

  • grant a number of votes in the governance of the Intelli Token.

The Standard Stream features a 100,000 Intelli allocation. It relates to the amount of tokens allocated in the first 2 years and is the base for the calculations for subsequent years.

Intelli Streams come in different Sizes, numbered from 1 to 50.

A Size 1 Intelli Stream delivers the Standard Stream, that is, 100,000 Intelli during the first two years. Each increment in Size reflects a linear increment in the amount of Intelli delivered in the first two years.

For instance, a Size 2 Intelli Stream delivers 200,000 Intelli and a Size 50 Intelli Stream delivers 5 Million Intelli during the first two years.

In general, the yearly allocation is:



    Stream for year n = Standard Stream * Size * 1.2 / ( n + 1)

The number of Intelli Tokens to be allocated during the period of one year as per the above formula are actually delivered on a daily basis, in equal daily payments throughout the year.

Voting Rights

Each Intelli Stream is entitled to as many votes as the numeric Size of the Intelli Stream. Therefore, each is entitled to as many votes as the sum of the Sizes of all Intelli Streams the Team Member may hold.
