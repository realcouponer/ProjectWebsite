The ALGO Economic Flow

REFERENCES



Trading Intelligence Marketplace

1 | The Superalgos Organization issues ALGO and conducts an MEO (Micro Exchange Offering) every time an Exchange is on-boarded, and set up with its own Superalgos Trading League.

2 | Investors (crypto holders) buy ALGO at the Exchange.

3 | They use ALGO to pay for Asset Managers' subscription services.

4 | Asset Managers acquire the Trading Intelligence at the Superalgos Network, subscribing to individual trading bots.




Financial Life Evolution

5A | Trading bots pay for the data they consume from Indicators and Sensors. Indicators pay for the data they consume from other Indicators and Sensors.

5B | All Financial Beings pay Node Operators for execution and storage services provided by the node.

5C | All Financial Beings pay their creators, including the Team who released them and other Teams that contributed to their evolution in previous iterations.

5D | By paying a time-to-live fee, Financial Beings prove their dependencies that they are committed to providing the service (staying alive) for a certain minimum amount of time.




Team Members

People and companies working for the Superalgos Organization in Software and Project Development (including Exchanges) are incentivized via ALGO Virtual Miners.




Competitions

ALGO is awarded as prize money to competition winners, incentivizing competitions as the engine driving evolution of Financial Beings.
