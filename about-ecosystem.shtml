The Intelli Ecosystem

A permissionless business hub

The Intelli Ecosystem is the network of economic relationships embodied by the activities of numerous stakeholders of the , participating in a joint business.

The main purpose of the ecosystem is to enable a decentralized, self-sustainable and self-regulating economic environment. Participants are exposed to business opportunities and the profits obtained through the ecosystem's output are systematically distributed and channeled to entities according to their contributions.

The is the medium of payment within the ecosystem, and the project's native token.

In the long run, the output of the Intelli Ecosystem will be a of automated asset management and trading services, to be exposed to the general public.

The ecosystem is composed of several stakeholders. We shall briefly introduce them in to provide context to deeper explanations coming afterward.

Stakeholders

The entitites participating in the joint enterprise

Superalgos Organization

The Superalgos Organization is a not-for-profit organization, with the main purpose to promote the . The organization aims to make trading technology readily available to ever-increasing numbers of market participants by lowering the barriers to entry as well as promoting open-source collaboration.

The Superalgos Organization is the legal entity representing the Superalgos Project in front of , entering legal agreements with the latter to ensure a meaningful and profitable business relationship for all parties involved.

Community

The Community is embodied by the strong collaboration of a heterogeneous group participating in the .

A subset of the Community counts professional and amateur traders, developers and analysts, organized in Teams. Their main functions are creating, developing, maintaining and promoting the evolution of , so we call Teams .

Teams may also contribute to the development and maintenance of the platform, becoming Team Members.

Teams are free to create any number of financial beings and are free to set the fees other beings need to pay to consume the products (datasets) and services (trading) they offer.

Any individual in the Community may create a Team, and people may participate in multiple Teams at the same time.

Teams participate in to earn cash prizes and acquire a reputation, which in turn may help them market their services once the is up and running.

Node Operators

Node operators are people or companies running and potentially offering hosting services for to run their Algorithms.

Everyone has the option to run Superalgos Nodes—that is, instances of the —which removes the need to trust other parties on the network with Algorithms code and/or ' API Keys.

In the future, Node Operators will be incentivized to participate in the through a discount offered by Exchanges, applicable to the volume generated by Superalgos Nodes in the Marketplace.

Exchanges

All crypto exchanges offering APIs to trade on their listed markets can participate in the . Superalgos is exchange-agnostic.

Exchanges play a crucial role in the ecosystem as they are the actor hosting and incentivizing —the ultimate driver of evolution—by offering significant prize money to winners.

They will also play an important role in incentivizing the actual use of the once the is operational: Exchanges will route discounts back to Node Operators, based on their trading volume.

Subscribers

In the future, people, companies, and institutions will be able to hire Asset Managers to trade on their behalf by subscribing to asset management services for a fee.

Subscribers will eventually become the main consumers in the ecosystem.

However, for this to happen, both the and need to evolve further, to enable a certain performance level in terms of the ecosystem's output.

Asset Managers

Asset managers will be companies or individuals in the network, providing financial advice/services to investors/subscribers. Their services may vary in terms of sophistication according to the size of capitals they manage.

Asset management services may be as elaborate as available elsewhere in the industry or as simple as an application connecting the subscription directly to .

Financial Beings

Financial beings are autonomous agents with a of their own, meaning that they live or die depending on their ability to produce value in the form of products (data) and services (trading), which they sell to obtain financial resources and guarantee their subsistence.

Financial life is a leading paradigm playing a fundamental role in balancing the ecosystem. Because need to pay for their living expenses, they strive to be as efficient as possible. Financial life naturally selects top performers and discards inefficient specimens.
