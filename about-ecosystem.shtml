The Superalgos Ecosystem

A permissionless business hub

The Superalgos Ecosystem is a network of economic relationships embodied by interdependent modules and their stakeholders participating in a joint business.

The main purpose of the ecosystem is to enable a decentralized, self-sustainable and self-regulating economic environment by which all participants are exposed to business opportunities and through which the profits obtained through the ecosystem's output as a joint enterprise are systematically distributed and channeled to participants according to their individual contributions.

The ALGO token is the medium of payment within the ecosystem, and the project's native token.

In the long run, the output of the joint business will be a marketplace of automated trading services, to be exposed to the general public.

The ecosystem is composed of several stakeholders. We shall briefly introduce them in order to provide context to deeper explanations coming afterwards.

Stakeholders

The entitites participating in the joint enterprise

Financial Beings

Financial beings are autonomous agents with a financial life of their own, meaning that the live or die depending on their ability to produce value in the form of products (data) and services (trading), which they sell to obtain financial resources and guarantee their subsistence.

Financial life is a leading paradigm playing a fundamental role in balancing the ecosystem. Because financial beings need to pay for their own living expenses, they strive to be as efficient as possible. Financial life naturally selects top performers and discards inefficient specimens.

Community

The community is the human component of the project, formed by a heterogeneous group of people counting mainly developers, traders and analysts, self-organizing in teams, which form organically through spontaneous relationships within the community.

Teams main functions are creating, developing, maintaining and promoting the evolution of financial beings. Teams may also contribute to the development and maintenance of the platform.

Teams are free to create any number of financial beings and –most times—are free to set the fees other beings need to pay to consume the products (datasets) and services they offer.

Any individual in the community may create a team, and people may participate in multiple teams at the same time.

Audience

The audience is people watching the developments of the Superalgos universe, following live competitions, consuming public functionality and potentially evaluating ways of getting involved at a deeper level.

This segment of the community creates demand for certain functionality and represents a target for competition sponsors.

Competition Hosts

Competition hosts are people, companies or institutions organizing trading competitions using the Superalgos Platform. The ecosystem is permissionless in the sense that allows anyone to organize all sorts of competitions.

We expect algorithmic trading competitions will eventually become as mainstream as e-sports, attracting important followings and growing into international structures, with regulatory bodies, associations and federations. When competitions grow to such dimensions, they will most likely become a substantial business and gain notable exposure.

Competition Sponsors

Competition sponsors are people, companies or brands supporting trading competitions by entering competition hosts' sponsorship programs.

They help finance competition events, enabling competitions to grow and attract more people through more attractive prize money incentives and broader exposure.

Exchanges

All kinds of crypto exchanges offering APIs to trade on their listed markets are able to participate in the ecosystem.

The platform is exchange-agnostic. The demand for trading with any specific exchange will drive the development of the required connectors by teams with an entrepreneurial mindset.

Subscribers

In the future, people, companies and institutions will be able to hire financial beings to trade on their behalf by subscribing to financial beings' services for a fee, through an automated trading services marketplace.

Subscribers will eventually become the main consumers in the ecosystem.

However, in order for this to happen, both the platform and financial beings need to evolve further, so as to enable a certain performance level in terms of the ecosystem's output.

Platform

The platform is the collection of software components that enable the project's vision. It provides the living environment for financial beings to exist, the arena for them to compete, and the marketplace for stakeholders to relate and transact with each other.

It is composed of the Cloud App which handles the cloud environment, the Canvas App which renders datasets in a timeline, a Master App running a number of modules providing frontend and backend functionality, and blockchain elements such as smart contracts.
