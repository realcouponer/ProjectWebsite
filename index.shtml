Evolution

Iteration Through Competition Cycles

Superalgos enables and incentivizes teams of developers, traders and analysts, both professionals and amateurs alike, to come together in teams and train trading bots that compete on a wide array of algorithmic trading competitions.

Every time a competition finishes, participants' source code is opened, resulting in the crowd forking winners' code and ideas, improving them and bringing the new generation back for the next round of competitions in the quest for fame, glory and hefty prizes.

The expected outcome is a relentless evolution of trading algorithms and a systematic increase in collective performance through each competition cycle.

Part of the intelligence resulting from these ongoing evolution cycles might eventually be absorbed by the platform.

After enough iterations, a wealth of strategies will have been explored and tested. Surviving evolutions of the original algorithms will construct the collective memory and experiences that feed and train superalgos.

In order to maximize evolution speed and range, the project will incentivize teams by hosting many types of official competitions in various formats, measuring different metrics, expanding into local, regional, national, continental and world events. In addition, it will open its technology for any organization to run unofficial competitions as well.

This constellation of competitions are opportunities for teams, designed to enlarge the development taskforce while accelerating trading strategies evolution and testing the widest possible strategies space in the shortest period of time possible.

Superalgo

A Superior Trading Intelligence

A superalgo is not just a high-performance trading algorithm. It is a trading intelligence that knows about all possible trading strategies, remembers what worked and what didn't in different circumstances, and learns from that experience. When trading, a superalgo, uses any combination of all known strategies, dynamically switching or combining them as needed.

Supermind

An Intelligent Collective

A supermind is a group of individuals acting together in ways that seem intelligent, as defined by Prof. Thomas W. Malone in his book Superminds: The Surprising Power of People and Computers Thinking Together.

Prof. Malone and his colleagues at the MIT Center for Collective Intelligence found that groups–like humans—have a measurable collective intelligence and identified specific factors correlated to it.

Superalgos aims to maximize it's collective intelligence in order to create a superalgo in the shortest time possible.

To expand our capabilities, we invite researchers in the fields of Collective Intelligence, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, Game Theory, Social Studies, Psychology and others to participate in the project at different levels.

There are opportunities to conduct studies and experiments, whenever those interventions may be conducive to increasing the project's efficiency or the collective intelligence of the supermind.

We also welcome professionals in the fields of law, governance, business, economy, communication, marketing, and other fields for which the project requires top resources to excel.
