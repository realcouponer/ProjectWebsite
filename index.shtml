Why We Compete

A glimpse into the existing model and the Superalgos alternative

The Status Quo

Investment companies keep trading technology in absolute secrecy. The assumption is made that this gives them an edge over other market participants. It is also assumed that lose performance when used too broadly. Even within a company, specialists work alone or in small groups. Evolution of trading intelligence is therefore capped by the intelligence of small groups.

Our Open Evolutionary Approach

A global collective creates Algorithms and puts them to compete for prize money, reputation and for fun! After each ends, Algorithms are open-sourced. The collective improves the best ones and puts the new generation back to compete again, unleashing a relentless evolutionary process. Algorithms losing performance are quickly replaced by improved evolutions.

Roadmap

The Path to Reorganizing Trading Intelligence

Project Kick-off: August 2017.

The stated with founder Luis Fernando Molina, a visionary entrepreneur, systems architect, with a track record in fintech developing mission-critical systems for banks. Together with his brother Julian, they set up a Core Team that helped deliver an MVP by October 2018.

The story of the inception of the project was documented by the brothers in a series of two articles published in Hacker Noon: Superalgos & The Trading Singularity, and Superalgos: Building a Trading Supermind.

Minimum Viable Product: Delivered, October 2018.

The MVP was delivered in pre-alpha stage in October 2018, consisting of a platform enabling running bots in the cloud and on the browser, in live trading, competition and backtesting modes. It included running basic competitions, handling users, teams and API exchange keys.

It also included several bots, with a sensor connected to Poloniex, several indicators and a trader. The platform featured a visualization engine rendering bots' actions and datasets over a graphic timeline. Debugging of code and strategies over the timeline was done directly in the browser.

Superalgos Network: Alpha version ETA Q4 2019.

The Superalgos Network is a P2P Network consisting of running both part of the and .

The main purpose of the Superalgos Network is to remove trust requirements by allowing all stakeholders to run their own instances of the Superalgos Platform.

Anyone may download the Superalgos Node and deploy it in their own server, becoming a Node Operator.

Node Operators may run the software for their own use or offer a hosted solution for users who chose to trust the operator (with their Algorithms' code and / or Exchange API Keys).

Superalgos Nodes work mostly on a stand-alone, independent basis in terms of their core activities, but may speak with each other to consume data and services from other nodes, and to relay relevant network information.

The data and services offered among nodes may be black-boxed (offered on an input / output basis, without revealing how the output is obtained) and sold for micro fees, payable in in between Algorithms. The transactions may include indicator data, signals or any form of trading intelligence that lives distributed across the network.

Exchanges shall give back discounts on transaction fees charged on the network's bundled trading volume, constituting a significant incentive to run Algorithms within the network instead of running them otherwise.
