What is Superalgos?

Open-Source Crypto Trading Bots

Superalgos is a free, open-source and trustless crypto trading bots and crypto markets intelligence suite. It integrates and leverages all important aspects of trading intelligence: data-mining and visualization, strategy testing, trading execution, and full trading automation. The visual experience makes Superalgos accessible to both technical and non-technical users.
