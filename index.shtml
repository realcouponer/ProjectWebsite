What is Superalgos?

The Ultimate Open-Source Crypto Trading Bots Platform

Superalgos is an easy, free, and open-source crypto trading bots platform and crypto markets intelligence suite. It integrates and leverages all crucial aspects of trading intelligence: data-mining and visualization, strategy testing, trading execution, and full trading automation. The visual experience makes Superalgos accessible to both technical and non-technical users.
