• Superalgos


    An open source project developing and implementing
    a framework to disrupt the reigning active investments model.

  • The Status Quo

    No one shares their algorithms
    or know-how.

    • Investment companies keep trading technology in absolute secrecy.
    • The assumption is made that this gives them an edge over other market participants.
    • It is also assumed that algorithms lose performance when used too broadly.
    • Even within a company, specialists work alone or in small groups.
    • Evolution of trading intelligence is therefore capped by the intelligence of small groups.

  • Our Approach

    Challenge the silos
    with a global collective.

    • A global collective creates trading algorithms → uncapped group size!
    • Algorithms participate in competitions for prize money and reputation → shift in incentives!
    • After each competition, algorithms are open-sourced → collective knowledge levelled upwards!
    • The collective improves the best algorithms and puts them to compete again → rapid evolution!

  • The Outcome

    A collective trading intelligence alongside closed-source providers,
    coexisting on a decentralized network hosting a Trading Intelligence Marketplace.

    • Our open-source trading intelligence is distributed across a trustless peer-to-peer network.
    • The network is open to traditional investment companies and other black-boxed intelligences.
    • A global marketplace provides various asset management services to investors of all calibers.
    • The system is glued with significant monetary incentives to participate in the network.

  • Superalgos Trading League

    A multi-layer league system designed to drive evolution
    towards trading increasingly important value in assets.

    • Minor leagues run short competitions, with minimal capital requirements and small prizes.
    • Major leagues run longer events, trade more capital and offer significant prize money.
    • Algo-makers access state-of-the-art tech after each event, keeping entry barriers low.
    • Teams winning an event get enough prize money to cover the capital req. for the next league.

  • Partnership with Exchanges

    Exchanges take a leading role hosting, sponsoring
    and marketing trading competitions.

    • Exchanges host competitions to drive trading volume to their own businesses.
    • They sponsor significant prize money to incentivize algo-maker teams to compete.
    • Price money comes at no cost to exchanges as it comes from standard volume discounts.
    • Exchanges market competitions to their clients, onboarding whole communities to Superalgos.

  • Trading Intelligence Marketplace

    As the network grows,
    intelligence is put at the service of all people.

    • Investors of all calibers may rent asset management services offered throughout the network.
    • Asset managers range from professionals to automated solutions renting algos from algo-makers.
    • Algo-makers counting traders, developers and data analysist are paid for the use of their algos.
    • Constant evolution guarantees diversity and prompt replacement of algos that lose their edge.

  • Superalgos Network

    The whole system runs distributed on a trustless, decentralized,
    permissionless peer-to-peer network.

    • Stakeholders run their own instance of the platform on a node, removing all trust requirements.
    • Nodes may offer data, trading and asset management services in a permissionless manner.
    • Services may be black-boxed without revealing how the output is obtained and sold for fees.
    • Services available throughout the network may be leveraged and combined in infinite manners.

  • Network Incentives

    The network leverages its bundled trading volume
    to obtain significant cash-back discounts from exchanges.

    • Exchanges compete for customers within the Superalgos Network to capture trading volume.
    • They run special nodes federating regular nodes driving trading volume to their exchange.
    • Nodes get a cash-back payment obtained from transaction-fee discounts on volume they drive.
    • This is an incentive to trade from within the network as no benefits are obtained otherwise.

  • The Main Challenge

    Bootstrapping the project
    until it snowballs!

    • The project is wired around two parallel positive feedback loops that accelerate growth.
    • Both loops incentivize algo-makers, the key to an ever-growing collective intelligence.

    More algo-makers → more competitors → more volume → more prize money → more algo-makers!

    More algo-makers → more intelligence → more performance → more demand for rentals → more algo-makers!

  • Go to Market Strategy

    We leverage the partnership with exchanges
    to onboard their communities, one by one as the project advances.

    1. Activate traders with the Strategizer, a tool to customize pre-programmed algos via a GUI.
    2. Onboard first exchange to market Superalgos within their clients → deal signed!
    3. Host micro token offering at the Exchange and launch alpha-stage competitions.
    4. Repeat with other exchanges, targeting bigger ones as the network matures.
